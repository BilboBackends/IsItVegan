DishTune

Privacy Policy

Effective July 13, 2026

DishTune (dishtune.com) helps you find vegan-friendly dishes at restaurants. This page explains what information the site handles and why. The short version: browsing needs no account and collects nothing that identifies you; creating an account stores your email and the content you choose to save or post, and nothing is ever sold or used for advertising.

Browsing without an account

  • Restaurant and dish data is served as static files. We run no advertising and no tracking cookies.
  • Favorites and thumbs-up/down made without an account are stored in your own browser (local storage) and as anonymous counts.
  • If you tap "near me", your location is used in your browser to sort results by distance. It is not stored or sent to us.
  • If you search an address, the query is geocoded by OpenStreetMap's Nominatim service, subject to the OSMF privacy policy. Map tiles are served by CARTO/OpenStreetMap.

If you create an account

  • We store your email address (from Google sign-in or an email sign-in link), an optional public username you choose, and the content you create: favorites, votes, and notes/comments.
  • Notes and your chosen username are publicly visible to other visitors. Your email address is never shown publicly.
  • Sign-in emails are delivered via Resend. Accounts and content are stored with Supabase. Google sign-in is handled by Google OAuth. The site is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. These providers process data only to run the features described here.

What we don't do

  • No selling or renting of personal information — to anyone, ever.
  • No advertising networks, no cross-site tracking.

Deleting your data

You can delete your own notes in the app at any time. To delete your account and everything attached to it, email us and we'll remove it.
